Quick Rules

  • Set: Cards of the same number (e.g., three 5s)
  • Run: Cards in sequence (e.g., 3-4-5-6)
  • Skip cards and wilds can be used to complete phases
  • Lowest score wins
  • First player to complete Phase 10 wins
